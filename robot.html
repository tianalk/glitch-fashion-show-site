GL/TCH is a testament to the power of aesthetics to shape our mores.

To GL/TCH is to take a 2026 technology — generative AI, face recognition, surveillance, recommendation algorithms, cryptocurrency, online betting — and explore how aesthetics can warp how we view this technology.

Designers will choose if they are using their aesthetics to show the FEAR that we should have in engaging with these strange new technologies — or to show the UTOPIA that will be possible and/or desired.

The goal of GL/TCH is not to litigate which technologies and which policies have the most merit, i.e., our goal is not to determine whether the surveillance state will be worth its hassle. Rather, GL/TCH's role is to sharpen our ability to see how aesthetics — especially body and garment aesthetics — can be created, utilized, and manipulated to push forward ideas about technology. Which aesthetics are the most useful in convincing us in either direction about surveillance's sexiness or horror?
